About the Series

As District 106's Public Relations Manager, Leo Smith presents this groundbreaking series designed to equip Toastmasters with cutting-edge AI tools to enhance club experiences and supercharge recruitment through public relations.

Each one-hour monthly session is beginner-friendly, using free AI tools (Grok, ChatGPT free tier, Google Gemini) and emphasizing collaborative, social activities that align perfectly with Toastmasters' community spirit.

ü§ù In Collaboration with PR Pros Toastmasters Club

This series is proudly sponsored and collaboratively developed with **PR Pros Toastmasters Club**, District 106's premiere club dedicated to empowering communicators and leaders with cutting-edge public relations strategies. Leveraging their specialized expertise, this partnership ensures you gain unparalleled insights and practical skills from seasoned professionals.

Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting out, PR Pros Toastmasters Club offers a supportive environment to grow your skills and network with like-minded individuals. Explore more about their community: prpros.toastmastersclubs.org
