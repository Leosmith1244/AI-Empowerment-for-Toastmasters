Session 6

AI for Guest and Member Follow-Ups

March 12, 2026
6:00 PM - 7:00 PM (Central)
Zoom Meeting

About This Session

This session focuses on leveraging AI to **personalize outreach** for both retaining existing members and converting guests into active members. You'll learn how to draft tailored messages that strengthen community ties and boost engagement.

What You'll Learn (Objectives)

  • Use AI to draft tailored follow-up messages.
  • Strengthen community ties and improve member retention.

Session Agenda (Activities)

10 min

Importance of Follow-Ups & AI

Discussion on the critical role of effective follow-ups in Toastmasters and how AI can optimize this process.

30 min

Personalized Email Drafting

In pairs, prompt AI for follow-up emails (e.g., "Draft a warm email to a guest who attended a club meeting").

15 min

Role-Play & Feedback

Groups role-play delivering messages, sharing feedback on tone, personalization, and overall impact of the AI-generated content.

5 min

Wrap-up & Engagement Homework

Challenge to draft a personalized follow-up for a recent guest or member using AI.

Resources & Preparation

Tools Needed

  • Free AI for drafting messages (e.g., Grok, ChatGPT free tier, Google Gemini)
  • Email platforms (for mock-ups, no live sending required)
  • Smartphone or laptop

Handouts

Examples of effective follow-up strategies and AI prompt guides for different scenarios will be provided.
