About This Session

In this crucial session, you'll learn how to **prepare AI-assisted responses for potential PR challenges**, such as low turnout at events or negative feedback. The goal is to develop quick, effective strategies to protect your district's reputation and maintain public trust.

What You'll Learn (Objectives)

  • Utilize AI to draft rapid and effective crisis responses.
  • Develop strategies to protect district reputation and manage challenging PR scenarios.

Session Agenda (Activities)
