AI for Event Promotion and Planning

February 12, 2026
6:00 PM - 7:00 PM (Central)
Zoom Meeting

About This Session

This session focuses on leveraging AI to **streamline the promotion and agenda creation** for District 106 events, such as workshops or contests. You'll learn how to craft irresistible event materials to boost attendance and engagement.

What You'll Learn (Objectives)

  • Design compelling event outlines and promotional blurbs.
  • Utilize AI to enhance attendance and engagement for district events.

Session Agenda (Activities)

10 min

Event Planning with AI Intro

Overview of how AI can assist in the various stages of event planning and promotion.

30 min

Workshop Outline & Promo Design

Teams use AI to design a workshop outline and promotional blurb (e.g., "Create a 30-min session on leadership with a flyer blurb").

15 min

Collaborative Plan Refinement

Groups present their event plans, refining them collaboratively for potential district use, focusing on clarity and appeal.

5 min

Wrap-up & Actionable Homework

Challenge to apply AI to draft a preliminary plan for a future club or district event.

Resources & Preparation

Tools Needed

  • Free AI for outlines and text generation (e.g., Grok, ChatGPT free tier, Google Gemini)
  • Google Docs or similar (for group editing)
  • Smartphone or laptop

Handouts

Examples of effective event promotion materials and AI prompts for event planning will be provided.
