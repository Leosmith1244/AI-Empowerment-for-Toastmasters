About This Session

This session explores how to use AI to **track Toastmasters' buzz** in the media and on social platforms, identifying key PR opportunities such as influencer collaborations or trending topics. The goal is to boost district visibility by monitoring trends and building strategic partnerships.

What You'll Learn (Objectives)

  • Utilize AI to monitor media mentions and identify PR trends.
  • Develop strategies for building partnerships and increasing district visibility.

Session Agenda (Activities)

10 min

Intro to Media Monitoring

Understanding the importance of tracking media and social conversations for PR success.

30 min

AI for Insight Generation

Groups query AI for media insights (e.g., "Summarize X posts about public speaking in our region").

15 min

PR Opportunity Board

Teams create a "PR opportunity board" (e.g., a shared document), presenting collaboration ideas to the group for discussion and refinement.

5 min

Wrap-up & Strategic Homework

Challenge to identify a potential PR opportunity for your club or district using AI-driven research.

Resources & Preparation

Tools Needed

  • Free AI for summaries and web search (e.g., Grok, ChatGPT free tier, Google Gemini)
  • Smartphone or laptop

Handouts

Guides on effective media monitoring techniques and examples of successful PR collaborations.
