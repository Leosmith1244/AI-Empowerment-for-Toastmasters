About This Session

This opening session introduces AI as a powerful tool to amplify District 106's PR efforts, from branding to outreach. We'll explore how AI can transform your club's public relations strategies with minimal technical expertise required.

What You'll Learn

  • Understand AI basics and how they apply to Toastmasters PR
  • Explore free tools for common PR tasks like crafting club promotions
  • See live demos of AI generating PR content
  • Collaborate with peers to brainstorm AI applications for your club

Session Agenda

10 min

Introduction & Demo

See Grok generate a club slogan (e.g., "Create a catchy tagline for a Toastmasters open house")

30 min

Hands-on Group Work

In small groups, members will prompt AI for PR ideas (e.g., event themes) and share in a roundtable, brainstorming how to use them district-wide.

15 min

Sharing & Q&A

Groups present their findings and discuss applications for their clubs.

5 min

Wrap-up & Homework

Try one AI prompt related to a club PR task; share next session.
