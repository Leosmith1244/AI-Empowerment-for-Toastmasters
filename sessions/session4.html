AI for Social Media Recruitment

About This Session

This session, presented by Leo Smith and his team in collaboration with **PR Pros Toastmasters Club**, focuses on leveraging AI to create **engaging social media content** that attracts guests and helps grow your Toastmasters clubs. Learn how to design posts that resonate with your target audience, with insights from PR Pros Toastmasters Club on effective digital communication.

What You'll Learn (Objectives)

  • Design compelling X (formerly Twitter) or LinkedIn posts.
  • Craft social media content that reflects District 106’s vibrant community.

Session Agenda (Activities)

10 min

Social Media & AI Intro

Quick overview of effective social media strategies for recruitment and how AI fits in.

30 min

AI Campaign Creation

Groups prompt AI for a social media campaign (e.g., "Write 3 X posts promoting a district workshop, with hashtags").

15 min

Mock Posting & Voting

Teams simulate posting their campaigns, and the group votes on the most engaging and effective content in a fun share-out.

5 min

Wrap-up & Homework

Experiment with creating a social media post for your club using AI; share insights next session.

Resources & Preparation

Tools Needed

  • Free AI for post ideas (e.g., Grok, ChatGPT free tier, Google Gemini)
  • X/LinkedIn (for mock-ups, no live posting required)
  • Smartphone or laptop

Handouts

Examples of successful social media prompts and content ideas will be provided.
