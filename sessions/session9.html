About This Session

This concluding session of the AI-PR Empowerment Series, presented by Leo Smith and his team in collaboration with **PR Pros Toastmasters Club**, focuses on **responsible AI use** and envisioning a positive future for District 106’s Public Relations. We'll ensure AI strategies align with Toastmasters’ core values and plan for ongoing PR success, benefiting from PR Pros Toastmasters Club's ethical communication leadership.

What You'll Learn (Objectives)

  • Ensure AI use aligns with Toastmasters’ values.
  • Plan ongoing, ethical PR strategies for the district.

Session Agenda (Activities)

10 min

Recap & Ethical AI Introduction

Brief recap of the series and introduction to the principles of ethical AI in PR.

30 min

AI-Powered PR Manifesto

Groups draft an AI-powered PR manifesto (e.g., "Vision for District 106’s 2026 recruitment campaign") using AI tools.

15 min

Ethics Roundtable & Pledge

A roundtable discussion on AI ethics (e.g., avoiding over-automation), culminating in a group pledge to use AI responsibly within Toastmasters.

5 min

Wrap-up & Future Vision

Final thoughts on the series and a vision for continued AI integration in District 106 PR efforts.

Resources & Preparation

Tools Needed

  • Free AI for drafting (e.g., Grok, ChatGPT free tier, Google Gemini)
  • Group polling tools (for ethics discussions)
  • Smartphone or laptop

Handouts

Guidelines for ethical AI use in PR and templates for creating a PR vision statement.

Why You'll Love This (Why Appealing)

This session unites members in a shared vision, producing a district-wide PR plan while reinforcing community values, ensuring responsible and impactful use of AI moving forward.
