About This Session

Building on Session 1, this session focuses on the art of **prompt engineering**. You'll learn how to write clear, specific, and effective prompts to get tailored PR content from AI, ensuring reliable and creative outputs for your club's needs.

What You'll Learn (Objectives)

  • Write clear prompts for reliable, creative outputs (e.g., social posts, emails)
  • Understand the nuances of AI interaction for optimal PR content generation

Session Agenda (Activities)

10 min

Recap & Prompt Basics

Quick review of Session 1 and introduction to prompt engineering principles.

30 min

Paired Prompt Challenge

In pairs, craft prompts for various PR scenarios (e.g., "Write a 100-word newsletter blurb for a district speech contest").

15 min

Critique & Refine

Groups swap their AI-generated outputs, providing constructive feedback on clarity, impact, and prompt effectiveness in a lively Q&A.

5 min

Wrap-up & Next Steps

Discussion on key takeaways and preparation for Session 3's focus on storytelling.

Resources & Preparation

Tools Needed

  • Free AI chatbots (Grok, ChatGPT free tier, Google Gemini)
  • Smartphone or laptop

Handouts

A comprehensive prompt template handout will be provided, along with examples for various PR needs.
